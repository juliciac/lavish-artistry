MEET YOUR ARTIST

Lavish Skin Artistry is a privately, black woman-owned business. My name is Rickia Coates and I am a Licensed Esthetician in Maryland. My mission is to empower every client to feel confident and radiant in their own skin. As a black woman-owned business, I celebrate diversity and individuality through expert full body waxing, sugaring services, ombre powder brows, and personalized skincare solutions. I am dedicated to providing a luxurious and inclusive experience, using the highest quality products and techniques to enhance your natural beauty and promote self-love. My goal is to create a welcoming space where you can partake in self-care, feel confident, be authentically you and can embrace your most lavish self.
