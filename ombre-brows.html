Policies

Please read the policies below to ensure you understand what you’re signing up for. Booking an appointment is an automatic acknowledgment of and agreement to these policies, so please read carefully.

Medical History

It is crucial that you complete the medical history form truthfully, as not everyone is medically eligible for ombre brows. Lavish Skin Artistry LLC will not be held liable for effects stemming from false or omitted medical history.

A consultation is required for anyone over age 55. Services will not be performed on anyone over age 65.

Payment/Non-refundable Deposit

A nonrefundable deposit is required to reserve your session. It will be applied toward your balance. Please book only if you are serious. Balance is due at session’s end. Accepted: card, cash, Cash App, Venmo, Zelle. No refunds.

Rescheduling

One reschedule is allowed within 6 weeks. A second reschedule requires a new deposit. Cancellations or being unserviceable under 48 hours' notice incur a 50% fee. No deposit transfer. Emergencies may be considered.

Guests

Due to space and appointment duration, no guests — including children — are allowed.

Appointment Length

Sessions take 2.5–3 hours on average. Touch-ups: ~1 hour. Timing may vary based on mapping or pigment absorption. Please keep your entire day open.

What are the Benefits of Ombre Brows?

Tired of daily brow makeup? Ombre Powder Brows enhance your look with minimal effort. Benefits include:

  • Minimally invasive, minimal pain
  • Creates a full, defined look
  • Shapes and defines brows
  • Can correct previous microblading
  • Works well on oily skin
  • Suitable for thinner or mature skin

How Long Do Ombre Brows Last?

Ombre brows typically fade within 24–36 months. Touch-ups are available every 6 months if desired. Your artist will assess when it's time for a refresh!

How to Prepare for an Ombre Brows Appointment

Prep is key to great results. Follow these guidelines before your appointment:

  • No caffeine the day of your appointment
  • No alcohol 24 hours prior
  • No painkillers or blood thinners 24 hours prior
  • No fish oil or Vitamin E one week before
  • No fillers/injections 3 weeks prior
  • No chemical peels near brows 2 weeks prior
  • No antibiotics 24 hours prior

Ombre Brows Aftercare

Let your brows rest for 24 hours, keeping them dry. After that, wash gently morning and night, and apply the aftercare cream provided.

Avoid the following until fully healed:

  • Makeup on brows
  • Scratching or picking
  • Exfoliants on brows
  • Sweating for 10 days
  • Swimming or saunas for 10 days
  • UV or tanning beds

Always consult your artist for any additional instructions.
